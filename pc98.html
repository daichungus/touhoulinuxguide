Choosing an emulator

There exists several emulators of the PC-98 platform, many of which work very well on Windows.

However, they tend to have issues when running on Linux with Wine. As such, this guide will only cover two emulators:

  • Neko Project II Kai
  • DOSBox-X
A list of PC-98 emulators can be found here.
